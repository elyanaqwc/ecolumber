In retrospect

Established in 1989

ECO LUMBER SDN BHD

has steadily carved out a distinguished presence in the property market and timber logging concessions. With a keen eye for sustainable practices and a commitment to quality, Eco Lumber has successfully completed numerous projects that underscore its expertise and dedication to excellence.

In 2007, recognizing the growing demand for premium timber products, Eco Lumber expanded its horizons by establishing an export division. This strategic move enabled the company to penetrate lucrative markets in the Middle East, South Africa, and Europe, offering an array of high-quality sawn timber and timber products. Today, Eco Lumber stands as a testament to innovation and resilience, delivering 'Nature's Best, With Care' to its global clientele.

Our Services

Export of Timber

In 2007, Eco Lumber Sdn. Bhd. expanded to include the export of high-quality sawn timber. We serve clients in the Middle East, South Africa, and Europe, providing premium timber that meets international standards for quality and sustainability. Our export division ensures meticulous inspection and certification, guaranteeing reliable, timely, and efficient delivery to our global clientele.

Logging

Eco Lumber Sdn. Bhd. has been committed to sustainable logging practices since 1989. Our operations prioritize minimizing environmental impact while maximizing yield. Using state-of-the-art equipment and strict environmental standards, we balance resource extraction with forest conservation, ensuring efficiency and responsibility in our logging activities.

Timber Products

Eco Lumber Sdn. Bhd. offers a diverse range of high-quality timber products, from construction-grade lumber to fine timber for furniture. We source our timber responsibly, adhering to sustainable forestry practices, and provide various wood species to meet market needs. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our products consistently exceed industry standards.
